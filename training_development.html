Course Modules: Your Path to HR Mastery

Click on any module to see the practical activities included!

Understand the strategic role of HR as a business partner. We'll cover the complete employee lifecycle, key HR metrics (like attrition and time-to-hire), and the structure of a high-performing HR department.

Learn to attract and hire top talent. This module covers everything from writing compelling job descriptions and sourcing on platforms like LinkedIn and Naukri, to conducting interviews using the STAR method and rolling out offers.

Hands-on Practice: You'll screen real resumes and conduct mock interviews!

First impressions matter. Master the art of making new hires feel welcome and prepared. You'll learn to manage all Day 1 formalities, create engaging induction programs, and fill out critical statutory forms (PF, ESIC, etc.).

Gain confidence in the most critical HR function. This deep dive covers the essentials of Indian law, including PF, ESI, Gratuity, and the Maternity Benefit Act. You'll also learn to structure a salary slip and understand CTC vs. in-hand salary.

Hands-on Practice: Calculate a complete salary breakup with all statutory deductions.

Learn how to nurture talent and build a positive work environment. We'll cover the end-to-end performance appraisal cycle, how to give constructive feedback, manage Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs), and handle employee grievances with empathy and professionalism.

Capstone Project: Become the HR Manager

Put your new skills to the test! In your final project, you will manage the entire lifecycle of a fictional employee.

  • Draft their Job Description.
  • Create their Offer Letter and Salary Structure.
  • Design their 1-week Onboarding Plan.
  • Conduct their Performance Appraisal.
  • Process their resignation and calculate the Final Settlement.

This project proves you have the integrated skills to walk into any HR department and add value immediately.
